About

AuditBull was started by Magnataur, a cybersecurity and compliance consulting firm that serves regulated B2B and B2B2C platform companies. AuditBull is a member of a new class of business process solutions known as ChatOps.​

ChatOps delivers a 10x improvement in organizational communication and process visibility in the same way that DevOps has for infrastructure delivery​

AuditBull leverages this new business paradigm which leverages tools that your teams are already familiar with to make execution of the cybersecurity and compliance obligations as simple, straightforward, and impactful as possible​
