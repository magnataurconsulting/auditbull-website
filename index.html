AuditBull allows scaling organizations to obtain and retain customer trust by orchestrating Trust Services in the tools your team already knows how to use, all while paying for itself with bottom line savings

The Scenario

Understanding the Customer Trust “Catch-22”

  • It is difficult to anticipate when Security and Compliance will become important to winning new business
  • Altering existing processes to accommodate compliance requirements is frustrating and time-consuming
  • Obtaining SOC2, HIPAA, or HITRUST certification takes significant time to prepare for

The Pitch

As a scaling start-up or mid size enterprise, it is difficult to implement cybersecurity and compliance processes that can pass SOC2, HIPAA, and HITRUST audit requirements without breaking the bank or risking product development slowdown.
AuditBull is built to eliminate waste and frustration using our Trust ChatBot to orchestrate and track compliance activities.
Unlike other compliance platforms, we make doing the work simple, not just tracking it.
